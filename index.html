About Ohyrus

Built for Start-Ups.
Proven with Enterprises.

Founded in October 2011, Ohyrus Inc. is an international digital media company headquartered in New York, USA. We specialise in helping SMEs and high-growth start-ups harness the power of the internet through smart, data-driven products and services.

In most cases, our work makes efficient use of our core competency within data mining — enabling clients to extract real competitive advantage from the web.

Our founders have extensive experience starting successful ventures after studying at top-tier universities and working with the best firms nationally and internationally. Our team spans talented software engineers, designers, e-business strategists, content writers, and internet marketers.

Our relatively small size means we deliver quick, personalised service — something larger agencies simply can't match. We know how hard it is for start-ups and SMEs to navigate the best online strategy, and that's exactly where we thrive.

Case Study 01 — Web Engineering

Price Comparison Search Engine

Built a comprehensive travel price comparison portal allowing users to search, study, and compare real-time data aggregated from 24 different hotels and airline search engines. Users could make bookings directly through the platform after comparing options.

The system relied on an extensive, custom-built data scraping pipeline using multi-CURL for timely accumulation of accurate pricing data at scale, supplemented by Google AdSense and affiliate marketing integration.

Case Study 02 — Internet Marketing

YoorStore Social Media Campaign

Designed and executed a comprehensive social media marketing campaign for YoorStore — a mobile-based C2C Classifieds Marketplace. The campaign spanned Facebook, Twitter, and additional growth channels to drive user acquisition and marketplace activity.

Ohyrus coordinated content strategy, community engagement, and paid amplification to build momentum for the platform's early user base across multiple markets.

Social Media Marketing Facebook Ads Twitter Growth Strategy
Case Study 03 — Brand Identity

Optimum Photography — Complete Brand Identity

Designed the complete visual brand identity for Optimum Photography — including logo, colour palette, typography system, and supporting brand collateral. The result was a cohesive, professional identity that positioned the studio for growth.

The project demonstrates Ohyrus's capability to go beyond code — delivering end-to-end digital and brand presence for clients who need a complete solution.
